About Stroke Guard

This is a web program that uses machine learning networks in order to look at a picture of the user and determine if they are at risk of getting a stroke. Approximately 750,000 people in the United States get a stroke per year. The symptons could be hard to detect, so this program aims to make it easier to figure it out.
